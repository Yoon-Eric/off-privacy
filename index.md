# Privacy Policy for Off

**Effective date: June 20, 2026**

Off ("the app", "we", "us") is a focus tool that helps you block distracting
apps on your own iPhone using Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls) framework.
Your privacy is simple to describe because the app is built to keep everything
on your device.

## Information we collect

**We do not collect any personal information.** The app has no account system,
no login, and no server. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data.

## Data that stays on your device

To function, the app stores the following locally on your iPhone only:

- **Your app/website selections** for what to block. These are represented by
  opaque tokens provided by Apple's Family Controls framework. They are stored
  on-device in a private app group container and are never transmitted anywhere.
- **Your settings and preferences** (such as session and notification
  preferences), stored locally on your device.

This information never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is
removed if you delete the app.

## Screen Time / Family Controls

The app uses Apple's Family Controls, Device Activity, and Managed Settings
frameworks to block distracting apps **on the same device, for yourself**. This
access is governed by Apple's system permission prompt, which you can grant or
revoke at any time in iOS Settings. Apple does not share the contents of your
Screen Time activity with us, and we do not request or receive it.

## Third parties

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, or
network requests. No data is shared with any third party.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.
